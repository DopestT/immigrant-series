If You’re Not Native, You’re an Immigrant

A documentary series on labor, land, and American history.

This project examines how America developed, who provided the labor, who benefited, and how historical narratives change after the work is done.

What This Series Covers

  • Indigenous presence before colonization
  • Enslaved African labor and wealth creation
  • Asian immigrant infrastructure building
  • Border changes and reclassification
  • Recurring cycles of labor and exclusion

Purpose

This series presents historical context. It does not assign personal guilt or advocate hostility. Its goal is accuracy, understanding, and informed discussion.

History doesn’t change. Stories do.
